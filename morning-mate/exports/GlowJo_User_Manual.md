# GlowJo — User Manual
**Version 1.0 | getglowjo.com**

---

## What is GlowJo?

GlowJo is a morning routine app for children aged 3–12. It turns the daily school-morning rush into a fun, star-earning game that kids actually want to play — reducing stress, nagging, and late starts for the whole family.

Kids tap through their morning tasks one by one, earn stars, hear encouraging voices, and work towards a weekly reward chosen by you.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Visit the App
Go to **getglowjo.com** on any phone, tablet, or computer. No app store download needed — it runs in the browser.

### 2. Sign Up
Tap **Start Free** on the home page. Enter your email and choose a password to create your free account.

> **Free trial**: Visitors get **3 free mornings** before being asked to sign up. Signing up is free — no credit card required.

### 3. First-Time Setup (Onboarding)
When you first open the app you will be asked:

| Question | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Child's name | e.g. Emma |
| Age | 3–12 |
| School start time | Used to show a countdown clock |
| Weekly reward | Choose from the list or type your own |
| Tasks to include | Tick the ones that apply |

Tap **Let's Go** and the app is ready for your child.

## The Morning Routine (Child's Screen)

### Starting the Routine
Hand the phone or tablet to your child. They will see:
- A sleeping sun ☀️ in the centre
- Their name and a "Ready to start?" message
- Their total stars ⭐ and streak 🔥 at the top

Tap the big circle to wake up Sunny and begin.

### Completing Tasks
Each task appears one at a time. The child taps the big button when they have completed it. Sunny the mascot reacts with praise, and a voice message plays to encourage them.

**Star ring**: A golden ring around the button fills up as tasks are completed.

**Skip**: If a task doesn't apply today (e.g. no shower on weekends), tap the small **Skip** button below the main button.

### Weekly Star Strip
Below the task button, five circles represent the school week (Monday–Friday). Each circle fills with a ⭐ when the child completes their morning routine on that day.

### Finishing the Routine
When all tasks are done, Sunny celebrates! The screen transitions to the **Win Screen**.

## Parent Dashboard

Go to **getglowjo.com/parent** (or tap **Parent Dashboard** on the win screen or app menu).

### Child Profiles
Tap the **pencil icon** to edit your child's profile at any time:
- Change their name, age, or school time
- Pick or change the **weekly reward** (see section below)
- Toggle which morning tasks are included

Tap **Add Child** (＋) to set up a second child.

### Choosing a Weekly Reward
When editing a child's profile, you will see a grid of 12 preset rewards to tap:

🍦 Ice Cream Friday · 🍕 Pizza Night · 🎬 Movie Night  
🎮 Extra Game Time · 🛝 Park Trip · ⭐ Choose Dinner  
🍫 Chocolate Treat · 🎨 Arts & Crafts · 🧸 New Toy/Book  
🏊 Swimming Trip · 🎉 Friend Sleepover · 🍔 Burger Night

Or type your own reward in the box below — e.g. "Trampoline Park" or "Stay up 30 mins late".

The chosen reward is shown to the child on the weekly progress bar during their morning routine.

### Parents' Voice *(GlowJo subscribers only)*
Record your own voice for each task. When the child reaches that task, they hear your voice instead of the built-in text-to-speech.

- Tap 🎙️ **Record** next to any task
- Speak your message (e.g. "Come on sweetheart, time to brush those teeth!")
- Tap **Stop** — it saves automatically
- Tap ▶ **Play** to hear it back
- Tap **Delete** to remove and re-record

### Tonight's Prep Checklist 🌙
A nightly checklist for parents to get organised the evening before. Available to all users.

Default tasks:
1. Set out school clothes
2. Pack school bag
3. Fill water bottle
4. Prepare lunch / snacks
5. Charge tablet / devices
6. Sign any letters or forms

Tap any task to tick it off. Ticks **reset automatically each morning** so the list is fresh every night. Tap **+** to add your own custom tasks — these carry over every night.

## Tips for Using with Kids

- **Set a routine time**: Open GlowJo at the same time every morning so it becomes habit.
- **Let them hold the phone**: Ownership matters. Kids engage more when they are in control.
- **Celebrate the win screen**: Make a fuss when they finish — the app cheers, you cheer too.
- **Use the weekly reward**: Talk about it each morning ("Remember, if you do this all week we get Pizza Night!").
- **Record Parents' Voice**: Kids respond better to a familiar voice. Even a short 5-second message works wonders.
- **Tonight's Prep**: Do the checklist the night before — a prepared morning is a calmer morning.

## Troubleshooting

**The app won't play sound**  
Most browsers block audio until the user taps something. Once your child taps the start button, sound should play. Check your phone is not on silent.

**My child's stars have reset**  
Stars are saved locally in the browser. If you cleared browser data or switched devices, log in to restore your saved progress from the account.

**The win screen shows 0 stars for the week**  
The weekly star strip resets each Monday. Stars already earned that week will show correctly after the next completed morning.

**I can't access the Parent Dashboard**  
You must be logged in to view the Parent Dashboard. Tap **Log In** at the top of the page.
