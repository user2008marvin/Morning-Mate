# GlowJo — User Manual
**Version 2.0 | getglowjo.com**

---

## What is GlowJo?

GlowJo is a routine app for children aged 3–12. It turns the daily school-morning rush — and the evening wind-down — into a fun, star-earning game that kids actually want to play. Less nagging, fewer late starts, calmer bedtimes.

Kids tap through their tasks one by one, earn stars, hear encouraging voices, and work towards a weekly reward chosen by you.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Visit the App
Go to **getglowjo.com** on any phone, tablet, or computer. No app store download needed — it runs in the browser.

### 2. Sign Up
Tap **Start Free** on the home page. Enter your email and choose a password.

> **Free trial**: Visitors get **3 free mornings** before being asked to sign up. Signing up is free — no credit card required.

### 3. First-Time Setup
When you first open the app you will be asked:

| Question | What to enter |
|---|---|
| Child's name | e.g. Emma |
| Age | 3–12 |
| School start time | Used to show a countdown clock |
| Weekly reward | Choose from the list or type your own |
| Tasks to include | Tick the ones that apply |

Tap **Let's Go** and the app is ready for your child.

## Morning Routine (Child's Screen)

### Starting the Routine
Hand the phone or tablet to your child. They will see:
- A sleeping sun ☀️ in the centre
- Their name and a "Ready to start?" message
- Their total stars ⭐ and streak 🔥 at the top

Tap the big circle to wake up Sunny and begin.

### Completing Tasks
Each task appears one at a time. The child taps the big button when they have completed it. Sunny reacts with praise and a voice message plays.

**Star ring**: A golden ring around the button fills up as tasks are completed.

**Skip**: If a task doesn't apply today, tap the small **Skip** button below the main button.

### Weekly Star Strip
Below the task button, seven circles represent the full week (Monday–Sunday). Each circle fills with a ⭐ when the child completes their routine on that day.

### Finishing the Routine
When all tasks are done, Sunny celebrates and the screen transitions to the **Win Screen**.

## Night Routine (Bedtime Mode)

Switch to Night Routine by tapping the **☀️ / 🌙 toggle** at the top of the app screen.

The app transforms into a dark, star-filled sky theme with calming music and gentler voice prompts to help children wind down.

### Night Tasks
The default bedtime routine includes:

| Task | |
|---|---|
| 🛁 Bath Time | Warm bath to relax |
| 🪥 Brush Teeth | Nighttime brush |
| 👕 Pyjamas On | Getting cosy |
| 📚 Story Time | Bedtime story (see below) |
| 💡 Lights Off | All done — sleep tight! |

### Story Time ✨
When the child reaches **Story Time**:
1. **First tap** — the story card slides in and Moony reads it aloud. A unique story plays for each day of the week (7 stories in English, 7 in Spanish), personalised with your child's name.
2. **Second tap** — once the story is finished, tap the task again to complete it and move on to Lights Off.

### Night Win Screen
After all bedtime tasks are done, Moony says goodnight and the win screen shows the full bedtime story so you can read it together if you like.

### Parent Dashboard — Night Mode
The **Start Routine** button in the Parent Dashboard automatically shows "🌙 Start Night Routine" or "🌅 Start Morning Routine" depending on which mode is currently active.

## Parent Dashboard

Go to **getglowjo.com/parent** (or tap **Parent Dashboard** on the win screen or app menu).

### Child Profiles
Tap the **pencil icon ✏️** to edit a child's profile:
- Change their name, age, or school time
- Pick or change the **weekly reward**
- Toggle which tasks are included
- Enable **SEND Mode** (see below)

Tap **Add Child** (＋) to set up additional children (up to your plan limit).

### SEND Mode
SEND Mode is designed for children with additional sensory or neurodivergent needs. When enabled for a child:
- Calmer, softer voice
- No flashing or visual overload
- No background music
- A **Now & Next** panel shows the current task and what comes after

Enable it from the child's edit screen in the Parent Dashboard.

### Choosing a Weekly Reward
When editing a child's profile, tap one of the 12 preset rewards or type your own:

🍦 Ice Cream Friday · 🍕 Pizza Night · 🎬 Movie Night  
🎮 Extra Game Time · 🛝 Park Trip · ⭐ Choose Dinner  
🍫 Chocolate Treat · 🎨 Arts & Crafts · 🧸 New Toy/Book  
🏊 Swimming Trip · 🎉 Friend Sleepover · 🍔 Burger Night

The chosen reward is shown to the child on the weekly progress bar during their routine.

### Parents' Voice *(GlowJo subscribers)*
Record your own voice for each task. When the child reaches that task, they hear your voice instead of the built-in text-to-speech.

- Tap 🎙️ **Record** next to any task
- Speak your message (e.g. "Come on sweetheart, time to brush those teeth!")
- Tap **Stop** — it saves automatically
- Tap ▶ **Play** to hear it back
- Tap 🗑 **Delete** to remove and re-record

### Tonight's Prep Checklist 🌙
A nightly checklist for parents to get organised the evening before. Available to all users.

Default tasks:
1. Set out school clothes
2. Pack school bag
3. Fill water bottle
4. Prepare lunch / snacks
5. Charge tablet / devices
6. Sign any letters or forms

Tap any task to tick it off. Ticks **reset automatically each morning**. Tap **+** to add your own custom tasks — these carry over every night.

### Week Star Chart
Each child's profile shows a Mon–Sun star chart. Tap **🖨️ Print Chart** to print it and stick it on the fridge.

## Tips for Using with Kids

- **Set a routine time**: Open GlowJo at the same time every morning and evening so it becomes habit.
- **Let them hold the phone**: Ownership matters. Kids engage more when they are in control.
- **Celebrate the win screen**: Make a fuss when they finish — the app cheers, you cheer too.
- **Use the weekly reward**: Mention it each morning ("Remember, if you do this all week we get Pizza Night!").
- **Record Parents' Voice**: Kids respond better to a familiar voice. Even a short 5-second message works wonders.
- **Tonight's Prep**: Do the checklist the night before — a prepared morning is a calmer morning.
- **Night Routine**: Start the bedtime flow 30 minutes before lights-out for best results.

## Troubleshooting

**The app won't play sound**  
Most browsers block audio until the user taps something. Once your child taps the start button, sound should play. Check your phone is not on silent.

**My child's stars have reset**  
Stars are saved locally in the browser. If you cleared browser data or switched devices, log in to restore your saved progress.

**The win screen shows 0 stars for the week**  
The weekly star strip resets each Monday. Stars earned that week will show correctly after the next completed routine.

**I can't access the Parent Dashboard**  
You must be logged in. Tap **Log In** at the top of the page.

**The Story Time story is the same every night**  
GlowJo has 7 different stories (one per day of the week). The story changes each evening at midnight.
